Our Services

Total Water Filtration

When water is pumped from the wells and tube-wells, all the impurities in the water such as sludge, dust, grease, taste, discoloration, odor, calcium and algae are removed by the Backwash process and delivered directly to the water tank. It can filter the whole water needed for a home.

Water Purifier

Normal Water containing Bacteria, viruses, solids, salts, discoloration, iron, calcium, fat, chlorine, taste, odor, etc., is treated using the world's best and simplest, Reverse osmosis technology, in which water is purified by splitting into two and hence provides fresh water.

Leak Proof

Leakage on terraces is a problem during the rainy season in many places. Leak proofing technology is the best solution to such problems. Leak proofing technology allows water to flow freely on the terrace and keep the terrace clean.

Automatic Water Level Controller

The use of a water level controller is to measure the water level in the water tank and prevent overflow. The automatic water level controller fills the tank with water when the water level in the tank drops below a certain limit and automatically turns off the motor when it is full.

Automatic Hand Sanitizer

In today’s situation hand sanitizing has become a part of lifestyle. We have a responsibility to protect ourselves and our community from viruses. Automatic hand sanitizer mainly used at offices, shops and shopping malls in which sanitizing can be done without touching the bottle.

Stainless Steel Water Tank

Stainless steel water tanks are the best choice to store water safely. Stainless steel water tanks can provide a solution to the health problems caused by the constant use of water from plastic water tanks. It is much easier to clean comparing with plastic tanks. Hence pure water can be used without plastic waste.
